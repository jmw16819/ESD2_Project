WeFlow Pressure Monitor

Welcome to the WeFlow Pressure Monitor! Here you can keep track of the state of the WeFlow, a water leak detection system. Below, water pressure data in PSI from three different sections of piping will be displayed alongside messages about the state of each section. Should a potential leak be detected in one of the sections, that section will automatically close, and the button corresponding to that section below can be used to reopen it.
